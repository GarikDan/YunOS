2.1.2 The main purposes we collect above personal information are: a. to guarantee the provision of our services (for example, when you create an Alibaba Group account, we have to collect your account name, your name, password and email address to ensure your login and the security of your account; when you purchase a product or service through e-commerce platform of Alibaba Group on YunOS, we have to collect the information of your Alibaba Group account to guarantee you a secure transaction and accurate service; in order to push you the weather forecasting information, we have to collect your geological location; when you backup your contacts, messages and other important data to the cloud through Cloud Space, we have to save the above data you have backed up; when you use the Anti-theft of Security Numen (may be renamed “Security” later), we have to collect your geological location.); b. to improve our products and services, thus help us meet your needs better and provide you with customized services (for example, in order to discover your application preferences, we may collect the preinstallation, uninstallation and usage duration of the applications in your phone launcher. We may also collect the usage of application interface and button, but we will not collect your specific personal information, such as comments, photos, etc.), thus to help us recommend you the applications meet your needs better). Otherwise, your experience with our products or services will be comparatively poor.
